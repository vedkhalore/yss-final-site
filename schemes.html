Yuva Shakti Sangathan - Schemes

Mission Skill 360

1-year paid National Skill Fellowship for fresh graduates, allowing them to work on community, tech, startup projects while earning ₹15,000 per month. Collaboration with local industries to create Skill Labs in every district. Young entrepreneurs under 30 pay zero stamp duty and registration fees for business startups.

Sakhi Suraksha Grid

GPS-enabled safe route app that shows verified safe roads, live police patrol tracking, and instant distress video calling to nearby police. Paid Return to Work Fellowship for women who left jobs due to maternity, offering retraining and part-time remote work.

Healthy Bharat Card

AI-based predictive health check once a year for every citizen free of cost, detecting early signs of lifestyle diseases. Youth Health Corps: Trained volunteers who promote mental health awareness, first aid, and basic health screening in schools and colleges.

Local to Global

Every district gets an export support cell for local artisans, farmers, MSMEs with help in branding, digital marketing, and connecting to international markets. Market Without Middleman: Farmers and artisans twice a month have access directly in urban areas.

Sneh Ashray Yojana

Providing safe, nurturing, and well-equipped homes for the elderly and orphaned children with proper shelter, nutritious meals, healthcare, education, and emotional wellbeing in a dignified environment. These homes promote a sense of belonging, community, and security, with trained caregivers, recreational facilities, and vocational training.

Karz Mukt Jeevan Yojana

“Fair Credit, Desh ka Spirit”. Credit Satya App: Real-time loan interest & EMI calculator. Alerts people of hidden charges or fraud apps. All moneylenders must have a government license to operate. Borrowers can verify license status on the Credit Satya App.

Green Guardian Program

Youth-led city and village teams to monitor air and water quality with open public dashboards. Paid internships for planting and maintaining urban forests.
