Our Manifesto

We the Yuva Shakti Sangathan pledge;

  • To empower every youth with skills, dignity and opportunities.
  • To create a society where safety, equality and respect for women are nonnegotiable.
  • To promote innovation in education and bridge the digital divide.
  • To strengthen local economies and help Indian products reach the world.
  • To protect our environment through active youth participation.
  • To ensure transparent governance through citizen driven policy feedback systems.

We are not here to promise freebies. We are here to promise a future worth building together.

Actions taken during our last Ruling Term

  • Under the “Bharat Udyog Yojana” – Approved 50,000 low-interest loans for SMEs, creating 5 lakh new jobs.
  • Under the “Aarogya Bharat Card” – Covered 12 crore families with ₹10 lakh health insurance each.
  • Reduced child malnutrition by 20% through Poshan Shakti Yojana.
  • Provided fortified meals to 5 crore children and pregnant women through Poshan Shakti Yojana, reducing malnutrition by 25%.
  • “Digital Gram Sankalp” – Connected 5 lakh villages with high-speed internet.
  • Sakhi Suraksha Yojana – Installed AI-enabled safety devices in 1,000 cities and launched a 24x7 women’s helpline.
